Welcome!

Gwinnett County Septic Tank Cleaning and Installation Company

With over 25 years of experience in Metro-Atlanta Septic Service , JC Septic Service is the among the best in the business when it comes to septic drainfield or septic tank fieldline repairs. We use top rated materials such as the Infiltrator Chamber Septic Systems: which is considered by many to be the best in the septic repair industry. We refuse to cut any corner when it comes to the proper diagnosis and repair of your septic system. We use compact equipment to protect your property and landscaping during the repair or service of you septic system. We also offer alternative septic systems like aerobic septic tanks and high capacity septic leachfields to save your property and save space.
